About Me

I'm a passionate multimedia journalist pursuing my MA in Journalism and Mass Communication at SRM Institute of Science and Technology. With expertise in long-form writing, research, and AI evaluation, I believe in the power of storytelling across all mediums.

When I'm not researching or writing, you'll find me exploring the intersection of technology and media, crafting stories that matter, or diving deep into documentary filmmaking and multimedia production.
